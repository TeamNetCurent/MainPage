How Volta Rover Works

An AI-powered, self-optimizing solar energy system

Intelligent Solar Tracking

The Volta Rover utilizes tilted mirrors to reflect sunlight onto high-efficiency photovoltaic cells. An AI-powered system calculates the optimal incidence of sunlight exposure in any given area, maximizing energy capture throughout the day.

24/7 Energy Availability

What happens during the night? Our AI-powered system uses light sensors to differentiate between day and night. The extra power stored within the capacitor is utilized to power homes when sunlight is unavailable.

Dynamic Energy Management

This system is dynamic and uses energy efficiently. Electricity is only delivered when required by the user, allowing the capacitor to recharge during low-demand periods. This intelligent distribution extends system longevity and reliability.

Advanced Photovoltaic Conversion

Photovoltaic cells convert sunlight directly into electricity. When light hits the cell's semiconductor material (usually silicon), it energizes electrons and frees them, creating an electric current. The cell's design with positive and negative layers creates an electric field that directs this electron flow, generating usable power.

Financial Aspect

Cost-effective solution with dual-market potential

Dual-Market Solution

NetCurrent presents a dual-market solution, addressing critical energy resilience for governments and accessible green power for consumers.

For public entities, it serves as a rapid-deployment, AI-optimized, external power source to secure essential services during grid failures or emergencies, ensuring constant energy where it is most needed.

For individual consumers, it solves the key barriers to solar adoption: its compact, automated design eliminates the need for large, fixed surface areas and is both portable and robust against weather, making reliable, clean energy feasible for those who cannot install traditional panels or farms.
